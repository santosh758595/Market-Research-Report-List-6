<p><strong>The UV-Vis Spectrometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Beam</li><li>Double-Beam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV-Vis spectrometer market is categorized into two main types: single-beam and double-beam. Single-beam spectrometers measure light intensity from the sample against a reference, simplifying design and offering cost-effectiveness suitable for routine analysis. In contrast, double-beam spectrometers split the light into two paths, comparing the sample and reference simultaneously, improving accuracy and stability by compensating for fluctuations in light sources. Both types serve various applications in chemistry, biology, and material science, catering to specific analytical needs.</p></p>
