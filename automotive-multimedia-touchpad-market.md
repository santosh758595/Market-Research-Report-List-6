<p><p>Automotive Multimedia Touchpads are available in two main types - capacitive and resistive. Capacitive touchpads rely on the electrical properties of the human body to detect touch, offering a more sensitive and durable interface. Resistive touchpads work through pressure and are generally less sensitive but more cost-effective than capacitive touchpads. Both types are commonly used in automotive multimedia systems to control various functions such as navigation, entertainment, and communication.</p></p>
