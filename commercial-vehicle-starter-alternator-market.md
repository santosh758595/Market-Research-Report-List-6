<p><p>The commercial vehicle starter and alternator market comprises two primary components: starters and alternators. Starters are electric motors that initiate engine operation by rotating the crankshaft, ensuring the vehicle starts efficiently. Alternators, on the other hand, generate electrical power by converting mechanical energy from the engine, supplying the electrical system and recharging the battery while the engine runs. Both components are crucial for the reliability and functionality of commercial vehicles, influencing performance and maintenance costs in the automotive industry.</p></p>
