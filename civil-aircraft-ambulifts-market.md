<p><p>The civil aircraft ambulift market features two main types: SideBull and FrontBull. SideBull ambulifts are designed to approach aircraft from the side, providing efficient access to passenger doors for boarding and disembarking, making them ideal for regional jets and smaller aircraft. In contrast, FrontBull ambulifts are engineered to access the front of the aircraft, accommodating larger planes and enhancing loading processes. Both types prioritize safety and speed in transferring passengers with reduced mobility, improving overall airport service efficiency.</p></p>
