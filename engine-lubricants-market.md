<p><strong>The Engine Lubricants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-grade</li><li>Multi-grade</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Engine lubricants are categorized primarily into single-grade and multi-grade types. Single-grade lubricants, such as SAE 30 or SAE 40, maintain consistent viscosity at specific temperatures, making them suitable for stable operating conditions. In contrast, multi-grade lubricants, like 10W-30, offer a broader temperature range, providing optimal performance across varying conditions. They combine the properties of two grades, ensuring better engine protection during cold starts and efficient operation at high temperatures, thus accommodating diverse driving environments and enhancing engine longevity.</p></p>
