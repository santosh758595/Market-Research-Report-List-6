<p><p>The anti-biofilm wound dressing market comprises three main types: physical, chemical, and biological. Physical dressings create a protective barrier to hinder biofilm formation through properties like moisture control and surface texture. Chemical dressings utilize antimicrobial agents or polymers that disrupt biofilm structures and inhibit bacterial growth. Biological dressings incorporate living cells or biomolecules that promote healing while combating biofilm development. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing wound care and preventing infection, ultimately improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
