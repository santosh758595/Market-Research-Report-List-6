<p><p>Crampons are categorized into three main types based on their attachment systems. Hybrid crampons combine features of both step-in and strap-on designs, offering versatility for various boots. Step-in crampons utilize specific boot models with a precise fitting system for quick attachment and enhanced performance, ideal for technical climbing. Strap-on crampons use adjustable straps to secure to a broader range of footwear, making them user-friendly and suitable for hikers and casual mountaineers. Each type serves distinct user needs and activities.</p></p>
