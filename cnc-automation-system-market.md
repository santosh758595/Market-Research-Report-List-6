<p><p>The CNC automation system market encompasses various machining processes, including turning, milling, and grinding. Turning involves rotating a workpiece against a cutting tool to create cylindrical shapes, ideal for shafts and round components. Milling utilizes rotating cutters to remove material from stationary workpieces, suitable for complex shapes and flat surfaces. Grinding employs abrasive wheels to achieve precise finishes and tight tolerances on materials. Each market segment addresses specific manufacturing needs, driving advancements in automation technology for improved efficiency and productivity.</p></p>
