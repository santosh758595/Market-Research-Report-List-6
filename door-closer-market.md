<p><strong>The Door Closer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Applied Door Closer</li><li>Concealed Door Closer</li><li>Floor Spring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The door closer market comprises three main types: surface applied door closers, concealed door closers, and floor springs. Surface applied door closers are mounted on the door's surface, making them visible and easy to install. Concealed door closers are installed within the door or frame, providing a cleaner aesthetic and protecting the mechanism from damage. Floor springs are embedded into the floor, offering a discreet solution ideal for heavy doors while allowing for varied control over closing speed and force.</p></p>
