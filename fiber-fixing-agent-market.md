<p><p>The Fiber Fixing Agent market includes three main types: </p><p>1. **Cationic Polymer Type** - These agents utilize positively charged polymers to bind negatively charged fibers, enhancing dye fixation and fabric retention during washing.</p><p>2. **Resin Type** - This type employs synthetic or natural resins to create a durable bond between fiber and dye, providing excellent wash and rub fastness.</p><p>3. **Cross-linking Reactive Type** - These agents form covalent bonds between fiber molecules, improving durability and stability while enhancing performance against environmental factors.</p></p>
