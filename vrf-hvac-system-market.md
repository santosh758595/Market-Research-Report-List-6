<p><p>The VRF (Variable Refrigerant Flow) HVAC system market is characterized by two primary branch pipe types: horizontal and vertical. Horizontal branch pipes are commonly used in low-rise buildings where space allows for lateral distribution of refrigerant, optimizing installation efficiency. In contrast, vertical branch pipes are ideal for high-rise structures, facilitating refrigerant flow through multiple floors and maximizing energy efficiency. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the versatility and performance of VRF systems in diverse building designs.</p></p>
