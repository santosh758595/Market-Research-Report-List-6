<p><p>The hot stamping machine market can be categorized into three main types based on the shape of the substrate and die interaction: Flat-Flat, Round-Flat, and Round-Round. Flat-Flat machines are designed for flat substrates, allowing for efficient stamping on surfaces such as paper or plastic sheets. Round-Flat machines cater to cylindrical objects with a flat stamping surface, ideal for items like bottles. Round-Round machines are suited for both objects and dies that are cylindrical, ensuring consistent quality on round surfaces.</p></p>
