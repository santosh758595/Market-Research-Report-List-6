<p><p>The Offline 3D Solder Paste Inspection Equipment market comprises two main types: laser-based and PMP-based systems. Laser-based 3D solder paste inspection utilizes laser triangulation technology to create accurate 3D models of solder paste application, ensuring precise measurement of thickness and volume. In contrast, PMP-based systems rely on a projector to illuminate the solder paste, capturing detailed images for analysis. Both types enhance quality control in electronics manufacturing by detecting inconsistencies and defects in solder paste application before component placement.</p></p>
