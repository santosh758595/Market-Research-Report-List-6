<p><ul><li>CZ Single Crystal Furnace</li><li>FZ Single Crystal Furnace</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Semiconductor Single Crystal Furnace market encompasses various types, primarily categorized into CZ (Czochralski) and FZ (Float Zone) single crystal furnaces. CZ furnaces utilize a method where a seed crystal is drawn upward through molten silicon, creating large single crystals ideal for semiconductor applications. In contrast, FZ furnaces leverage a zone melting technique, where a rod of silicon is melted and crystallized in a controlled environment, ensuring high purity and improved electronic properties. These technologies cater to the evolving demands of the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
