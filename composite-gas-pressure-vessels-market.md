<p><ul><li>Type III</li><li>Type IV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Composite gas pressure vessels are classified into Type III and Type IV based on their construction and materials. Type III vessels consist of a metal liner surrounded by a composite outer layer, offering a balance of lightweight design and robust pressure resistance. In contrast, Type IV vessels utilize a plastic liner fully wrapped with composite materials, providing even greater weight savings and higher resistance to permeation. These differences cater to diverse applications, particularly in automotive, aerospace, and industrial sectors, focusing on efficiency and safety.</p></p>
