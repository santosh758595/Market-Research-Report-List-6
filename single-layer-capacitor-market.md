<p><strong>The Single Layer Capacitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Mount</li><li>Through Hole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The single layer capacitor market can be categorized into two main types: surface mount and through hole capacitors. Surface mount capacitors are designed for mounting directly onto the surface of a PCB, offering advantages in terms of space efficiency and automated assembly. In contrast, through hole capacitors require a hole to be drilled in the PCB for mounting, providing greater mechanical stability and ease of repair. Both types cater to different application needs, influencing design decisions in electronic products.</p></p>
