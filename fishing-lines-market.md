<p><p>The fishing lines market primarily consists of two main types: braided and monofilament. Braided lines are made from multiple strands of synthetic fibers, offering high strength, sensitivity, and low stretch, making them ideal for heavy-duty fishing. In contrast, monofilament lines are single-stranded, providing greater flexibility and ease of use, often favored by beginners. Each type caters to different fishing needs, with braided lines excelling in durability and sensitivity, while monofilament lines offer affordability and versatility for various fishing techniques.</p></p>
