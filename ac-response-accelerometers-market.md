<p><strong>The AC Response Accelerometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Charge Mode Piezoelectric</li><li>Voltage Mode Piezoelectric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AC response accelerometers market includes two main types: charge mode piezoelectric and voltage mode piezoelectric accelerometers. Charge mode piezoelectric accelerometers generate an electrical charge in response to mechanical stress, providing high sensitivity and stability for dynamic applications. In contrast, voltage mode piezoelectric accelerometers convert mechanical motion into a voltage signal, offering easier integration and signal processing. Both types serve critical functions in vibration analysis and structural monitoring across various industries, including aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing.</p></p>
