<p><p>The Methyl Methacrylate market can be segmented based on production methods: the ACH method, Isobutylene method, and Ethylene method. The ACH method involves polymerization of methyl acrylate and methanol, producing high-purity methyl methacrylate. The Isobutylene method utilizes isobutylene and methacrylic acid, offering a more efficient route with lower environmental impact. The Ethylene method combines ethylene and methacrylic acid, focusing on cost-effectiveness and scalability. Each method caters to specific market demands, influencing pricing, production efficiency, and overall market dynamics.</p></p>
