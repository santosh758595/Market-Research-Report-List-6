The market is segmented into Singlemode and Multimode TAPs. Singlemode TAPs utilize a single light path, ideal for long-distance and high-bandwidth data transmission, while Multimode TAPs use multiple light paths, suitable for shorter distances and providing flexibility in network design. Both types serve specific networking needs, enhancing security, performance, and reliability in data monitoring infrastructures.</p></p>
