<p><p>The silica gel market is categorized into three main types: Type A, Type B, and Type C. Type A silica gel has a high adsorption capacity and is primarily used for humidity control in packaging and storage. Type B silica gel offers moderate adsorption and is often used in industrial applications. Type C silica gel features larger pore sizes, making it suitable for specific applications like gas separation. Each type serves distinct functions in moisture management across various industries.</p></p>
