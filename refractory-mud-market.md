<p><p>The refractory mud market encompasses various types of materials used for high-temperature applications. Clay refractory mud is characterized by its plasticity and thermal stability, making it suitable for traditional applications. High alumina refractory mud offers superior heat resistance and is used in industrial furnaces. Silicon refractory mud provides excellent thermal shock resistance, while magnesia refractory mud is known for its high melting point and resistance to basic slag. Each type serves specific needs in industries such as metallurgy, ceramics, and construction.</p></p>
