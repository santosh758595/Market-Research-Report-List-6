<p><p>The Automatic Container Handling Equipment market encompasses various technologies for efficient container movement. Automated Stacking Cranes (ASCs) facilitate high-density storage and retrieval in warehouses. Rubber Tired Gantry (RTG) cranes offer flexibility for stacking containers in yards. Ship-to-Shore (STS) cranes are designed for loading and unloading containers from ships. Automated Guided Vehicles (AGVs) transport containers autonomously within facilities. Other market types may include systems like rail-mounted gantry cranes and mobile harbor cranes, enhancing overall operational efficiency in container handling.</p></p>
