<p><p>The starch-based bioplastics market encompasses various processing methods, including blow molding, extrusion, injection molding, and others like thermoforming and foaming. Blow molding is used for creating hollow plastic products, while extrusion shapes materials into continuous profiles. Injection molding allows for precise manufacturing of complex parts. Thermoforming involves heating sheets of bioplastic and shaping them, while foaming creates lightweight materials by incorporating air. Together, these methods enable the production of sustainable and versatile packaging and consumer goods using renewable starch resources.</p></p>
