<p><ul><li>Viral Vectors</li><li>Cre-Dependant Expression System</li><li>Transgenic Animals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optogenetics market includes various types, such as viral vectors, Cre-dependent expression systems, and transgenic animals. Viral vectors are used to deliver light-responsive proteins into target cells, enabling precise control of neuronal activity. Cre-dependent expression systems allow for enhanced specificity in gene activation, providing targeted manipulation in genetic research. Transgenic animals are genetically modified to express optogenetic tools, offering in vivo model systems for studying neural circuits and behaviors, facilitating advancements in neuroscience and therapeutic applications.</p></p>
