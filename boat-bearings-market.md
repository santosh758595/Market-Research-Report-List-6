<p><strong>The Boat Bearings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rolling Bearing</li><li>Sliding Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The boat bearings market is primarily divided into two types: rolling bearings and sliding bearings. Rolling bearings utilize balls or rollers to reduce friction, providing efficiency and longevity, making them ideal for high-speed applications in marine engines. Sliding bearings, on the other hand, rely on a smooth surface for contact and are commonly used in applications requiring stability and load support, like propeller shafts. Each type serves distinct needs within the boating industry, influencing performance and maintenance characteristics.</p></p>
