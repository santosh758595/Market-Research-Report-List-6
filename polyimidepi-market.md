<p><p>The polyimide (PI) market is categorized into three types: aliphatic (linear polyimides), semi-aromatic, and aromatic. Aliphatic polyimides are known for their excellent flexibility and lower thermal stability. Semi-aromatic polyimides offer a balance of chemical resistance and mechanical properties, making them suitable for various applications. Aromatic polyimides are characterized by their superior thermal stability and mechanical strength, making them ideal for high-temperature applications. Each type serves specific industrial needs based on performance requirements.</p></p>
