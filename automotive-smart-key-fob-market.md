<p><p>Automotive Smart Key Fobs come in two main types: Passive Keyless Entry Systems (PKES) and Remote Keyless Entry Systems (RKES). PKES allow drivers to unlock and start their vehicles without needing to physically interact with the key fob. On the other hand, RKES require the driver to press a button on the key fob to unlock or start the vehicle. Both systems provide convenience and enhanced security for vehicle owners, with PKES being a more advanced and hands-free option.</p></p>
