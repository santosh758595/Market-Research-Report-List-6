<p><p>The automotive cabin filters market comprises three main types: particle filters, activated carbon filters, and electrostatic filters. Particle filters are designed to capture dust, pollen, and other airborne particles, enhancing air quality inside vehicles. Activated carbon filters go a step further by absorbing odors and harmful gases, providing a more pleasant driving experience. Electrostatic filters utilize an electric charge to attract and trap particles, offering superior filtration efficiency. Each type serves to improve cabin air quality, contributing to passenger comfort and health.</p></p>
