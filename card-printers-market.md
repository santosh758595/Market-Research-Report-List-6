<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Card Printers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct-to-Card Printers</li><li>Retransfer Printers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The card printer market primarily consists of two types: Direct-to-Card Printers and Retransfer Printers. Direct-to-Card Printers print images directly onto the card surface, offering a cost-effective solution for low to medium-volume printing with faster output. In contrast, Retransfer Printers use a two-step process, where the image is first printed onto a film, then transferred onto the card, producing superior image quality and durability, making them ideal for high-security applications and more complex card designs.</p></p>
