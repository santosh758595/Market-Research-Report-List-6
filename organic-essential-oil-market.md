Pure essential oils are extracted from specific plants through processes like distillation, ensuring they contain concentrated plant extracts without additives. In contrast, compound essential oils are blends of various essential oils and may include synthetic fragrances or carrier oils to achieve desired scents or therapeutic benefits. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences, with pure oils favored for their authenticity and compounds for versatility.</p></p>
