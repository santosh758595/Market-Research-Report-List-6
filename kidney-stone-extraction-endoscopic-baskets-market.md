<p><p>The kidney stone extraction endoscopic baskets market is primarily divided into two types: nitinol stone baskets and stainless steel stone baskets. Nitinol stone baskets are favored for their flexibility, allowing easier navigation through the urinary tract, and have a lower risk of breakage due to their shape-memory properties. In contrast, stainless steel stone baskets offer durability and resistance to wear, making them suitable for rigorous procedures. Both types are crucial for effective stone retrieval during endoscopic surgeries.</p></p>
