<p><strong>The Industrial Vacuum Evaporation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multiple Effect</li><li>Single Effect</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial vacuum evaporation system market is primarily segmented into two types: single effect and multiple effect systems. Single effect systems operate using one evaporation stage, effectively removing solvents but with lower efficiency and higher energy consumption. In contrast, multiple effect systems utilize several evaporation stages in a series, significantly increasing efficiency and reducing energy usage by reusing steam generated in previous stages. This makes multiple effect systems more cost-effective and environmentally friendly, leading to their growing popularity in various industries.</p></p>
