<p><ul><li>Resistance Sensor</li><li>Thermocouple Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Strap-on temperature sensors are non-invasive devices used to measure surface temperatures in various applications. The **Resistance Sensor** type, often based on RTDs or thermistors, detects temperature changes through variations in electrical resistance, offering high accuracy and stability, ideal for precise monitoring. The **Thermocouple Sensor** type measures temperature by generating a voltage proportional to the temperature difference between two junctions, known for its wide temperature range and durability, suitable for harsh environments. Both types are widely used in industries like manufacturing, HVAC, and energy.</p></p>
