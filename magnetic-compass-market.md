<p><p>The magnetic compass market comprises two primary types: direct-reading and distant-reading compasses. </p><p>Direct-reading compasses provide immediate and clear indications of magnetic north, allowing users to directly assess their orientation. These are typically used in activities like navigation and surveying.</p><p>Distant-reading compasses, on the other hand, are designed for long-range observations. They utilize additional instruments or features to provide bearings from a distance, making them suitable for applications in aviation and maritime navigation where precise positioning over greater distances is essential.</p></p>
