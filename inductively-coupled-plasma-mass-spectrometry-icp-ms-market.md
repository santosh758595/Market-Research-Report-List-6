<p><p>Inductively Coupled Plasma-Mass Spectrometry (ICP-MS) is a highly sensitive analytical technique used for detecting trace elements in various samples, including metals, environmental samples, and biological materials. This method employs an inductively coupled plasma to ionize the sample, allowing for the mass spectrometric analysis of its constituents. ICP-MS is renowned for its ability to analyze multiple elements simultaneously at very low concentrations, making it invaluable in fields such as environmental monitoring, pharmaceuticals, and food safety.</p><p>The ICP-MS market is experiencing substantial growth, driven by an increasing demand for reliable and rapid analytical techniques across various industries.
