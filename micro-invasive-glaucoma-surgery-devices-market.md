<p><p>Micro invasive glaucoma surgery devices market includes implants and blink-assisted devices. Implants are small devices that are surgically implanted to help control intraocular pressure in glaucoma patients. Blink-assisted devices, on the other hand, are externally applied devices that help in the drainage of aqueous humor to reduce eye pressure in glaucoma patients. Both types of devices offer minimally invasive options for glaucoma treatment, allowing for improved patient outcomes and reduced complications compared to traditional surgical methods.</p></p>
