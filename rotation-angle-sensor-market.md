<p><p>Rotation angle sensors are commonly categorized into two types: Brushless Sensors and Brushed Sensors. Brushless sensors use electronic circuits to detect rotation, eliminating the need for physical brushes, resulting in reduced wear and maintenance. On the other hand, Brushed sensors use physical brushes to make electrical contact, providing a simpler design but requiring more frequent maintenance. Both types of sensors are utilized in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and robotics for measuring and monitoring the rotation angle of shafts, motors, and other rotating components.</p></p>
