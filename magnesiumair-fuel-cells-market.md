<p><strong>The Magnesium–air Fuel Cells Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The Magnesium-air fuel cells market consists of two main types: rechargeable and non-rechargeable. Rechargeable magnesium-air fuel cells can be cycled multiple times by replenishing the magnesium anode and electrolyte, making them suitable for applications that require prolonged energy storage. In contrast, non-rechargeable cells are designed for single-use, where the magnesium is consumed in the electrochemical reaction. While non-rechargeable cells are often used in portable devices and backup power, rechargeable cells offer potential for more sustainable energy solutions and longer-term applications.</p></p>
