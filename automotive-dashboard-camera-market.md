<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Multi-Channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive dashboard camera market is divided into two primary types: single-channel and multi-channel cameras. Single-channel cameras capture video from one perspective, typically focused on the road ahead, making them cost-effective and user-friendly. In contrast, multi-channel cameras feature two or more lenses, enabling simultaneous recording from multiple angles, such as front, rear, and sides. This additional coverage enhances safety by providing a comprehensive view, making multi-channel systems increasingly popular among consumers seeking greater security and evidence in case of incidents.</p></p>
