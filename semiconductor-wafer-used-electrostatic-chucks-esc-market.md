The market features two main types of ESC: Coulomb type, which employs electrostatic forces from charged electrodes to grip wafers, ensuring stable positioning; and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) type, which utilizes a different mechanism for enhancing wafer adhesion and uniformity. Both types are crucial for optimizing production efficiency and precision in semiconductor fabrication.</p></p>
