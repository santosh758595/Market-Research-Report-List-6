<p><p>The plastic pallet market is primarily categorized into two types: High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE) and Polypropylene (PP). HDPE pallets are known for their durability, moisture resistance, and ability to withstand heavy loads, making them ideal for various industrial applications. Conversely, PP pallets are lightweight, flexible, and often used for lightweight shipping needs, offering good impact resistance. Both types cater to different sectors, driving innovation and sustainability in logistics and supply chain management while minimizing environmental impact.</p></p>
