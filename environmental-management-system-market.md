<p><strong>The Environmental Management System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ISO 14001</li><li>EMAS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Environmental Management Systems (EMS) primarily fall into two market types: ISO 14001 and EMAS. ISO 14001 is a globally recognized standard that helps organizations improve their environmental performance through efficient resource use and waste reduction. In contrast, the Eco-Management and Audit Scheme (EMAS) is a European Union initiative that reinforces ISO 14001 requirements with added transparency and compliance elements. Both frameworks drive sustainability efforts, enabling organizations to demonstrate commitment to environmental responsibility and enhance stakeholder trust.</p></p>
