<p><ul><li>Thermal Slide－off Debonding</li><li>Mechanical Debonding</li><li>Laser Debonding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Temporary Bonding Adhesive market encompasses three main types of debonding techniques: Thermal Slide-off Debonding, Mechanical Debonding, and Laser Debonding. Thermal Slide-off involves heating the adhesive to weaken the bond, allowing for easy removal. Mechanical Debonding uses physical force or tools to separate bonded substrates. Laser Debonding employs focused laser energy to precisely break the adhesive bond without damaging the surfaces. Each method offers unique advantages, catering to various applications in electronics, automotive, and other industries.</p></p>
