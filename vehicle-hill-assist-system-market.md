<p><p>The Vehicle Hill Assist System (VHAS) is a safety feature designed to prevent vehicles from rolling back when starting on an incline. This system enhances driver confidence and vehicle control in hilly terrains.</p><p>The Vehicle Hill Assist System Market is experiencing notable growth, driven by increasing sales of SUVs and vehicles equipped with advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS).
