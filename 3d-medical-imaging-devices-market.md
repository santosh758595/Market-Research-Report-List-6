<p><p>The 3D medical imaging devices market encompasses various technologies essential for accurate diagnosis and treatment. Ultrasound utilizes high-frequency sound waves to create real-time images of soft tissues and organs. Computed tomography (CT) combines X-ray images for detailed cross-sectional views, aiding in precise detection of abnormalities. Angiography, often performed with X-ray, visualizes blood vessels to identify blockages or malformations. Magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) uses magnetic fields and radio waves for high-resolution images of internal structures, enhancing disease assessment and planning.</p></p>
