<p><strong>The Road Surface Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The road surface sensor market can be categorized into two main types: active and passive sensors. Active sensors emit signals, such as infrared or microwave, and measure the reflections to detect surface conditions, providing real-time data on factors like temperature and moisture. In contrast, passive sensors rely on naturally occurring signals, such as heat from the road surface, to assess conditions. Both types play crucial roles in infrastructure monitoring, traffic management, and enhancing road safety by providing essential data for maintenance and planning.</p></p>
