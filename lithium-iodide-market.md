<p><p>The lithium iodide market is primarily segmented into two types: lithium iodide trihydrate and lithium iodide anhydrous. Lithium iodide trihydrate contains water molecules in its crystal structure, making it useful in various applications like moisture absorption and in pharmaceutical formulations. In contrast, lithium iodide anhydrous is devoid of water and is often utilized in more demanding chemical processes and as a reagent in laboratories. Both types serve distinct industries, including pharmaceuticals, electronics, and chemical manufacturing.</p></p>
