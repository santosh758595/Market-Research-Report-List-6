<p><p>The RF/Microwave Filter market encompasses various types, primarily including Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) filters, Bulk Acoustic Wave (BAW) filters, and other filter technologies. SAW filters utilize surface acoustic waves to process signals, making them compact and efficient for applications like mobile communications. BAW filters operate using bulk acoustic waves, offering high performance in frequency selectivity, ideal for 5G and other high-frequency applications. Other filters may include dielectric and waveguide filters, serving niche applications across telecommunications, aerospace, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
