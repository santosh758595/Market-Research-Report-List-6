<p><p>The Emergency Vacuum Splint and Mattress market consists of two key types: Emergency Vacuum Splints and Emergency Vacuum Mattresses. They conform to the body's shape, ensuring minimal movement and reducing further injury risk. Emergency Vacuum Mattresses, on the other hand, are used for full-body immobilization, particularly for spinal injuries. They mold around the patient’s body, enhancing comfort and protection while facilitating safe transportation to medical facilities.</p></p>
