<p><strong>The Dental Imaging Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intra-Oral Imaging Systems</li><li>Extra-Oral Imaging Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental imaging systems market is categorized into intraoral and extraoral imaging systems. Intraoral imaging systems involve devices that capture images from within the patient’s mouth, such as digital X-rays and intraoral cameras, providing detailed views for diagnostics. On the other hand, extraoral imaging systems, including panoramic and cone beam computed tomography, capture images from outside the mouth, offering broader views of the dental structure and surrounding tissues. Both types enhance diagnostic accuracy and treatment planning in dentistry.</p></p>
