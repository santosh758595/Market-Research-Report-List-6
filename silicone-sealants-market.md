<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Silicone Sealants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressure Sensitive</li><li>Radiation Curing</li><li>Room Temperature Vulcanizing</li><li>Thermoset</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silicone sealants market comprises various types: </p><p>1. **Pressure Sensitive** - These sealants adhere to surfaces upon application, offering quick bonding without heat or curing.</p><p>2. **Radiation Curing** - These use radiation sources for curing, providing rapid hardening and stability under extreme conditions.</p><p>3. **Room Temperature Vulcanizing (RTV)** - Curing at room temperature, RTV sealants are versatile and widely used for various applications.</p><p>4. **Thermoset** - These sealants harden irreversibly when heated, offering durability and resistance to high temperatures and chemicals.</p></p>
