<p><p>The cloud-based e-discovery software market comprises three main types: public, private, and hybrid clouds. Public cloud solutions offer shared resources over the internet, providing scalability and cost-effectiveness, ideal for smaller firms. Private clouds are dedicated to a single organization, offering enhanced security and compliance for sensitive data. Hybrid clouds combine both approaches, allowing organizations to balance between public and private services, optimizing flexibility, and resource management while addressing varying security and operational needs.</p></p>
