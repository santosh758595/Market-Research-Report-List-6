Rotating lamps provide broad visibility with a 360-degree light output, often used in emergency vehicles and industrial sites. Strongpoints are stationary lights that emit constant brightness, signaling a fixed hazard location. Flashing lights are designed to attract attention rapidly, typically employed in construction zones or temporary hazards. Together, they enhance safety and awareness in industrial environments.</p></p>
