Calcined limestone involves heating the stone to produce lime, which is used in various industrial processes. Crushed limestone is fragmented into smaller pieces for use in construction, agriculture, and other applications. Each type serves distinct applications based on purity and particle size, addressing diverse industry needs.</p></p>
