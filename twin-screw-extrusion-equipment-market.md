<p><strong>The Twin Screw Extrusion Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Meshing Type Extrusion Equipment</li><li>Non Meshing Type Extrusion Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The twin screw extrusion equipment market comprises two main types: meshing type and non-meshing type extrusion equipment. Meshing type equipment features interlocking screws that enhance mixing and compounding processes, allowing for better material homogenization and shape control. In contrast, non-meshing type equipment has parallel screws that facilitate material conveyance and are typically used for applications requiring lower shear forces. Both types cater to diverse industries, including plastics, food, and pharmaceuticals, each with distinct operational advantages and applications.</p></p>
