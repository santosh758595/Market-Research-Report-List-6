<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Electric Turbine</li><li>Axial Flow Electric Turbine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric turbine market comprises two primary types: centrifugal electric turbines and axial flow electric turbines. Centrifugal turbines utilize a rotating impeller to convert kinetic energy into pressure, making them suitable for high-pressure applications. In contrast, axial flow turbines direct fluid flow parallel to the turbine shaft, optimizing efficiency in large-scale operations, such as power generation. Both types serve critical roles in various industries, including energy, water management, and industrial processes, driving advancements in efficiency and sustainability.</p></p>
