<p><p>The dental fiberglass posts market is segmented into tapered, parallel, and other types. Tapered posts feature a conical shape that promotes better adaptation to the root canal, providing enhanced retention and stability. Parallel posts are uniform in width along their length, offering consistent support and are typically used in wider canals. Other types may include variations that combine features or use different materials. Each type is selected based on specific clinical requirements, ensuring optimal outcomes in dental restorations.</p></p>
