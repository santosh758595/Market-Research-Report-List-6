High Voltage ATS typically manages systems above 35 kV, crucial for large industrial facilities. Medium Voltage ATS operates between 1 kV and 35 kV, commonly used in commercial buildings and utility applications. Low Voltage ATS, functioning below 1 kV, is utilized in residential and small commercial settings for seamless power management and backup capabilities, ensuring reliability across diverse applications.</p></p>
