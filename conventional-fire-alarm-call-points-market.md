<p><p>The conventional fire alarm call points market comprises two primary types: glass element and plastic element call points. Glass element call points feature a breakable glass mechanism that activates the alarm when shattered, providing a reliable and traditional approach for emergency signaling. In contrast, plastic element call points utilize a push-button design, which is more durable and less prone to accidental activation. Both types enhance fire safety by ensuring quick and effective emergency responses in various environments.</p></p>
