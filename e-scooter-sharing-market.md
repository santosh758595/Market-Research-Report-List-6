<p><p>The e-scooter sharing market can be categorized mainly into dockless and station-based models. Dockless systems allow users to pick up and leave scooters anywhere within designated areas, promoting flexibility and convenience. In contrast, station-based models require users to rent scooters from specific docking stations and return them to the same or another station, providing more control over scooter placement and easier management. Both models aim to enhance urban mobility while addressing different user preferences and operational challenges.</p></p>
