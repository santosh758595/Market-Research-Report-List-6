<p><ul><li>Open Magnetic</li><li>Closed Magnetic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The saloon ignition coil market is categorized into two main types: open magnetic and closed magnetic. Open magnetic ignition coils utilize air as a medium for magnetic flux, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, but may be less efficient at higher engine speeds. In contrast, closed magnetic coils feature a magnetic core that enhances efficiency and performance by reducing energy losses. This distinction affects their application in various saloon vehicles, influencing factors such as power output, durability, and overall engine performance.</p></p>
