<p><p>RV precision reduction gears encompass two primary types: gear reducers and worm reducers. Gear reducers utilize a system of gears to provide a high torque output while minimizing speed, commonly used in robotics and automation. Worm reducers, on the other hand, employ a screw-like worm to achieve a higher reduction ratio, making them ideal for applications requiring precise movement and compact design. Both types are essential in enhancing the efficiency and performance of various mechanical systems.</p></p>
