<p><strong>The Commercial Flat Panel Antennas Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electronically Steered</li><li>Mechanically Steered</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Commercial Flat Panel Antennas market is categorized into two main types: electronically steered and mechanically steered antennas. Electronically steered antennas utilize phased array technology, allowing rapid beam direction changes without physical movement, enabling agile communication and tracking capabilities. In contrast, mechanically steered antennas rely on physical movement and hardware to adjust their orientation, offering a more traditional approach. Each type serves distinct applications, with electronically steered antennas often favored for modern, high-demand environments requiring precision and efficiency.</p></p>
