<p><p>The biodegradable recycled thermoplastics market includes three primary forms: flakes, pellets, and granulates. Flakes are small, flat pieces derived from recycling processes, ideal for further processing or blending. Pellets are molded or extruded shapes that facilitate easy handling and uniform melting during manufacturing. Granulates are larger particles used in various applications, providing versatility in extrusion and injection molding processes. Each type serves distinct roles in producing sustainable plastic products while promoting environmental responsibility through recycling.</p></p>
