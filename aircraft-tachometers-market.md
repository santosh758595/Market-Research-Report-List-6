<p><ul><li>Analog Type</li><li>Digital Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft tachometers market is primarily divided into two types: analog and digital. Analog tachometers utilize mechanical movements to display engine RPM on a dial, offering a traditional and straightforward readout. In contrast, digital tachometers use electronic sensors to provide precise, real-time data on engine performance, often featuring additional functionalities such as data logging and integration with other aircraft systems. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring optimal engine operation and enhancing pilot awareness during flight.</p></p>
