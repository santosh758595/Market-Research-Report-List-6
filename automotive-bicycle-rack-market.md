<p><p>The automotive bicycle rack market features three main types: hitch-mounted racks, roof-mounted racks, and trunk-mounted racks. Hitch-mounted racks attach to the vehicle's hitch receiver, offering easy loading and stability for multiple bikes. Roof-mounted racks secure bicycles on top, ideal for larger vehicles but may require lifting. Trunk-mounted racks fix to the rear of the vehicle, providing a budget-friendly solution with quick installation. Additionally, pickup carriers are specialized for transporting bikes in truck beds, catering to outdoor enthusiasts.</p></p>
