<p><ul><li>Creatinine Blood Test</li><li>Creatinine Urine Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The creatinine test device market comprises two primary types: the creatinine blood test and the creatinine urine test. The creatinine blood test measures serum creatinine levels to assess kidney function, often used in routine health screenings. In contrast, the creatinine urine test evaluates creatinine concentration in urine over a specified period, providing insights into kidney filtration efficiency and muscle metabolism. Both tests are essential for diagnosing kidney-related conditions and monitoring renal health, catering to different clinical needs within healthcare settings.</p></p>
