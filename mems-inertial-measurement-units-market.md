<p><p>MEMS Inertial Measurement Units (IMUs) are categorized based on the number of degrees of freedom they measure. 4DoF IMUs measure motion in four directions, typically forward, backward, left, and right. 6DoF IMUs add the ability to measure up and down motion. 9DoF IMUs further improve accuracy by adding orientation measurements such as pitch, roll, and yaw. 10DoF IMUs include an extra capability, such as air pressure sensing. Other markets may refer to specialized IMUs designed for specific applications like robotics, navigation, or virtual reality.</p></p>
