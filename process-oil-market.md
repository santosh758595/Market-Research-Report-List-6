<p><p>The process oil market comprises several types, each serving distinct applications. Naphthenic oils, derived from petroleum, are prized for their low viscosity and high solvency, making them ideal for lubricants and rubber manufacturing. Paraffinic oils offer good thermal stability and are often used in transformer oils and coatings. Aromatic oils, characterized by their high aromatic content, find use in industrial applications like rubber processing. Other oils include bio-based and synthetic options that cater to specialized markets, like environment-friendly formulations.</p></p>
