Saturated fatty acids, commonly found in tallow, are solid at room temperature and are often used in food and industrial applications. Monounsaturated fatty acids, with one double bond, are beneficial for heart health and are used in cooking oils. Polyunsaturated fatty acids, containing multiple double bonds, are essential for diets and are important for various industrial applications, including cosmetics and lubricants.</p></p>
