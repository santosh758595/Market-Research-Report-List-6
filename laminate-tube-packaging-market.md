<p><strong>The Laminate Tube Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ABL</li><li>PBL</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laminate tube packaging is categorized into two main types: Aluminum Blister Laminate (ABL) and Plastic Barrier Laminate (PBL). ABL consists of an aluminum layer sandwiched between plastic layers, offering excellent barrier properties against moisture and light, making it ideal for products like pharmaceuticals and cosmetics. PBL, on the other hand, utilizes a plastic substrate combined with barrier materials. This option is typically lighter and more flexible, catering to consumer goods such as lotions and sauces, while also being cost-effective and eco-friendly.</p></p>
