<p><p>Golf course management software can be categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and local-based solutions. Cloud-based software operates online, allowing users to access features and data from anywhere with an internet connection. It usually offers scalability, automatic updates, and lower upfront costs. Each type suits different operational needs and preferences.</p></p>
