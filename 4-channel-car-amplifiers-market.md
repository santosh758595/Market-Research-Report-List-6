<p><p>The 4-channel car amplifiers market is divided into OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) and aftermarket segments. OEM amplifiers are integrated into vehicles by manufacturers, designed for specific models to ensure optimal performance. In contrast, aftermarket amplifiers are sold separately and are typically installed by consumers or professionals to enhance existing audio systems. Aftermarket options offer customization and improved sound quality, catering to audiophiles and car enthusiasts, while OEM products focus on reliability and compatibility with factory-installed systems.</p></p>
