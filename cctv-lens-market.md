<p><p>The CCTV lens market consists of two main types: fixed focus lens and zoom lens. Fixed focus lens provides a set focal length, making it ideal for monitoring a specific area without the need for manual adjustments. On the other hand, zoom lens offers the flexibility to adjust the focal length, allowing for close-up views of objects at a distance. Both types cater to different surveillance needs, with fixed focus lens focusing on constant monitoring and zoom lens providing the ability to zoom in on specific details.</p></p>
