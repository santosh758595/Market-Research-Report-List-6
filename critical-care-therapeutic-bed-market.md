<p><p>The Critical Care Therapeutic Bed market includes Mechanical and Electric types. Electric beds, powered by motors, provide advanced features such as automated height adjustment, tilt, and Trendelenburg positioning, enhancing patient comfort and caregiver efficiency. Electric beds are preferred in intensive care units for their precision and ease of use, while mechanical beds suit less critical environments.</p></p>
