<p><p>Capacitive position sensors are used for measuring linear or angular position in various applications. Linear position sensors detect the linear displacement of an object, while angular position sensors measure the rotational movement of an object. Both types of sensors work by generating an electrical signal based on the changing capacitance between the sensor and the object being measured. This technology is widely utilized in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing for precise and reliable position sensing.</p></p>
