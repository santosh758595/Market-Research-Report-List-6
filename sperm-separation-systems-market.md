<p><ul><li>On-chip</li><li>Electrophoresis</li><li>Dielectrophoresis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sperm separation systems are essential in reproductive technologies, focusing on enhancing sperm quality for procedures like IVF. On-chip systems utilize microfluidic technology to separate sperm based on motility and morphology within compact devices, ensuring efficient processing. Electrophoresis leverages electric fields to segregate sperm by charge and size, providing high purity. Dielectrophoresis employs non-uniform electric fields to manipulate sperm based on their dielectric properties, allowing for selective capture and enrichment, thereby improving fertility treatment outcomes.</p></p>
