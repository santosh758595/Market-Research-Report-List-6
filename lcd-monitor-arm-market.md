<p><p>The LCD monitor arm market is segmented into three main types: keyhole mounts, fixture mounts, and wall-hanging designs. Keyhole mounts provide a secure and stable attachment, allowing for easy adjustments and repositioning. Fixture mounts, often used on desks or workstations, enable flexible positioning to enhance ergonomics and save workspace. Wall-hanging mounts offer an efficient way to save floor space, ideal for small areas, while providing various angles for optimal viewing. Each type caters to different user needs and environments.</p></p>
