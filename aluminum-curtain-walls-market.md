<p><ul><li>Stick-built</li><li>Semi-unitized</li><li>Unitized</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminum curtain wall market consists of three primary types: stick-built, semi-unitized, and unitized systems. Stick-built systems are constructed on-site, using individual components assembled together, offering flexibility in design but requiring more labor. Semi-unitized systems combine pre-assembled panels with on-site assembly for quicker installation and reduced labor costs. Unitized systems feature fully pre-fabricated panels shipped to the site for rapid installation, ensuring quality control and minimizing construction time. Each type caters to different project needs and budgets.</p></p>
