<p><ul><li>Emulsion SBR Latex</li><li>Solution SBR Latex</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SBR latex market comprises two main types: Emulsion SBR Latex and Solution SBR Latex. Emulsion SBR Latex is produced through emulsion polymerization, offering excellent binding properties, water resistance, and flexibility, making it ideal for applications like adhesives, paper coating, and carpet backing. Solution SBR Latex, created via solution polymerization, provides superior mechanical strength, abrasion resistance, and thermal stability, commonly used in high-performance tires and industrial rubber products. Both cater to diverse industries, driven by demand for durability and versatility.</p></p>
