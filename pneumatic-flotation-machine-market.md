<p><p>A Pneumatic Flotation Machine is a type of equipment used in mineral processing to separate valuable minerals from their ores through a process that utilizes air bubbles. This technology is particularly effective in separating fine particles, making it a preferred choice in various industries, including mining and wastewater treatment.
