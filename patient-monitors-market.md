<p><strong>The Patient Monitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pulse Oximeters</li><li>Spirometers</li><li>Capnographs</li><li>Peak flow meters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The patient monitors market encompasses various devices essential for assessing and managing patient health. Pulse oximeters measure oxygen saturation levels in the blood, aiding respiratory monitoring. Spirometers assess lung function by measuring air volume and flow, crucial for diagnosing respiratory conditions. Capnographs monitor carbon dioxide levels during ventilation, providing insights into respiratory efficiency. Peak flow meters evaluate the maximum speed of airflow during exhalation, helping manage asthma and other respiratory disorders. Each device serves a unique role in enhancing patient care.</p></p>
