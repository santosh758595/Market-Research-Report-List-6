<p><p>The Gear Hydraulic Pump market comprises various types, each serving distinct hydraulic applications. External Gear Pumps utilize two meshing gears to create fluid flow, known for their simplicity and reliability. Internal Gear Pumps feature an inner gear driving fluid through an outer gear, providing higher efficiency at low pressures. Gear Ring Pumps utilize a ring of gears to enhance flow and pressure control. Screw Spindle Pumps employ rotating screws to transfer fluids, ideal for handling viscous materials, offering versatility and precise flow rates in various industries.</p></p>
