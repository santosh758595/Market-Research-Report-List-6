<p><p>The air-cooled turbogenerator market primarily comprises two types: 2-pole air-cooled generators and 4-pole air-cooled generators. 2-pole generators are designed for high-speed operations, typically offering higher power output and efficiency, making them suitable for applications like combined cycle power plants. In contrast, 4-pole generators operate at lower speeds, providing smoother performance and stability, which is ideal for standard power generation applications. Both types leverage air cooling for efficient thermal management, ensuring reliable operation in various industrial environments.</p></p>
