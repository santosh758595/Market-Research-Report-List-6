<p><p>The bleached absorbent cotton market primarily consists of two types: virgin cotton and noil. Virgin cotton is produced from the first processing of cotton fibers, ensuring high purity and absorbency, making it ideal for medical and cosmetic applications. In contrast, noil cotton is derived from shorter fibers left over from the cotton-spinning process. While it has lower quality, it is often used for industrial applications and can be more cost-effective. Together, these types cater to diverse market needs.</p></p>
