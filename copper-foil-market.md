<p><ul><li>Rolled Copper Foil</li><li>Electrolytic Copper Foil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The copper foil market comprises primarily two types: rolled copper foil and electrolytic copper foil. Rolled copper foil is produced by mechanically rolling copper to desired thicknesses, offering high tensile strength and uniformity, making it suitable for various applications, including electronics and battery manufacturing. In contrast, electrolytic copper foil is created through an electroplating process, resulting in a thinner, more uniform product ideal for specialized uses in circuit boards and batteries. Both types are crucial for advancing technology in multiple industries.</p></p>
