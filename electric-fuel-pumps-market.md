<p><ul><li>Internal Electric Fuel Pumps</li><li>External Electric Fuel Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric fuel pumps market is primarily divided into two types: internal and external electric fuel pumps. Internal electric fuel pumps are typically submerged within the fuel tank and offer efficient fuel delivery directly to the engine, enhancing performance in automotive applications. Conversely, external electric fuel pumps are mounted outside the fuel tank, utilizing various mounting options to maintain optimal fuel pressure. Both types are crucial for modern fuel injection systems, catering to different vehicle designs and performance requirements.</p></p>
