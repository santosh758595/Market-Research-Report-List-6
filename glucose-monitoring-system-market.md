<p><p>The glucose monitoring system market consists of various components including sensors, transmitters and receivers, and integrated insulin pumps. Sensors are devices that detect glucose levels in the body, providing real-time data. Transmitters facilitate the wireless transfer of this data to receivers, which display the glucose readings for user convenience. Integrated insulin pumps combine continuous glucose monitoring with insulin delivery, allowing for automated regulation of blood sugar levels. Together, these components enhance diabetes management and improve patient outcomes.</p></p>
