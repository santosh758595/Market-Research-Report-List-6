<p><ul><li>Dried Cassava Pulp</li><li>Wet Cassava Pulp</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cassava pulp market is primarily divided into two types: dried cassava pulp and wet cassava pulp. Dried cassava pulp is processed to remove moisture, resulting in a lightweight product with a longer shelf life, ideal for animal feed and various industrial applications. Wet cassava pulp, on the other hand, retains a higher moisture content and is often used immediately in animal husbandry, fermentations, or as a raw material in food processing. Both types serve distinct purposes in different industries.</p></p>
