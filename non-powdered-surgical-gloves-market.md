<p><p>Non-powdered surgical gloves are medical-grade gloves designed to provide a barrier against contaminants while minimizing the risk of allergic reactions caused by latex powder. The market for non-powdered surgical gloves has gained traction due to the increasing demand for safer and more comfortable surgical options.</p><p>Market growth analysis indicates that the Non Powdered Surgical Gloves Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.70% during the forecast period. Additionally, the COVID-19 pandemic has exacerbated the demand for personal protective equipment, further boosting market expansion.</p><p>Latest trends include advancements in glove technology, such as enhanced grip and stretchability, and growing preference for sustainable and biodegradable materials.
