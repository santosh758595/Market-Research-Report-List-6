Nitrogenous flame retardants release non-combustible gases that dilute flammable vapors during combustion. Other market types include various organic and inorganic compounds that enhance fire resistance through different mechanisms. These alternatives are increasingly preferred due to environmental regulations and health concerns associated with halogenated options, offering safer and more sustainable solutions.</p></p>
