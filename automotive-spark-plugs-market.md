<p><strong>The Automotive Spark Plugs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Spark Plugs</li><li>Cold Spark Plugs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive spark plugs market is segmented into hot and cold spark plugs based on their thermal characteristics. Hot spark plugs retain heat to prevent fouling during low-speed driving and are suited for engines with lower performance demands. Cold spark plugs, on the other hand, dissipate heat more efficiently, making them ideal for high-performance engines that operate at higher temperatures. The choice between these spark plugs influences engine performance, fuel efficiency, and overall vehicle reliability.</p></p>
