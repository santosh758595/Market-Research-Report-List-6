<p><p>The automotive inside handle market comprises mainly two types: alloy die casting and plastic injection molding. Alloy die casting involves creating durable and sturdy handles from molten metal, ensuring long-lasting performance and a premium feel. In contrast, plastic injection molding utilizes thermoplastic materials to produce lightweight, cost-effective handles in various designs and colors. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and vehicle requirements, balancing aesthetics, functionality, and manufacturing efficiency in the automotive interior components sector.</p></p>
