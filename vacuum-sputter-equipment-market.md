<p><p>Vacuum sputter equipment market consists of three primary types: DC sputtering, RF sputtering, and others. DC sputtering uses direct current to create plasma for depositing thin films, ideal for conductive materials. RF sputtering employs radio frequency power, suitable for non-conductive materials or dielectrics, providing enhanced uniformity. Other methods may include magnetron and pulse sputtering, each catering to specific applications and material requirements. These technologies serve various industries, including electronics, optics, and manufacturing, driving innovation in thin film deposition.</p></p>
