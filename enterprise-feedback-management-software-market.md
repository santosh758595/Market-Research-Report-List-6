<p><p>Enterprise Feedback Management (EFM) software is categorized into two primary market types: on-premises and cloud-based. On-premises EFM solutions are installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater control over data security and customization but requiring higher upfront costs and IT management. Conversely, cloud-based EFM software is hosted remotely, offering scalability, easier updates, and lower initial investment. This model allows businesses to access feedback tools from anywhere and streamlines collaboration while minimizing the need for extensive IT infrastructure.</p></p>
