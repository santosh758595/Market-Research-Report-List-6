<p><p>The Natural Killer (NK) cells therapeutics market includes two primary types: NK cell therapies and NK cell-directed antibodies. NK cell therapies involve the enhancement, modification, or infusion of NK cells to target and destroy cancer cells. These therapies harness the innate ability of NK cells to recognize and kill malignant cells without prior sensitization. In contrast, NK cell-directed antibodies are designed to bind specifically to tumor-associated antigens, enhancing the activation and effectiveness of NK cells in targeting tumors.</p></p>
