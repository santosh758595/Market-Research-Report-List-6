<p><p>The sleep apnea treatment devices market includes various types aimed at alleviating obstructive sleep apnea. Positive Airway Pressure (PAP) devices, including CPAP, deliver continuous airflow to keep airways open. Oxygen devices provide supplemental oxygen to improve respiratory function during sleep. Oral appliances reposition the jaw or tongue to prevent airway obstruction. Adaptive Servo Ventilation (ASV) devices adjust pressure based on detected breathing patterns, offering tailored treatment for complex or central sleep apnea. Each device type addresses specific patient needs and conditions.</p></p>
