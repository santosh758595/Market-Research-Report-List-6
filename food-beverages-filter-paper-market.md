<p><p>The Food & Beverages Filter Paper market is divided into two main types: heat-sealable paper and non-heat-sealable paper. Heat-sealable paper is designed to create a sealed barrier when exposed to heat, enhancing freshness and preventing contamination, making it ideal for packaging. Non-heat-sealable paper, on the other hand, does not require heat for sealing and is often used in applications where a more straightforward, adhesive-free approach is preferred. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining food safety and quality.</p></p>
