<p><p>The fuse box market comprises several types, including cartridge fuse panels, aluminum wiring electrical panels, and breaker panels. Cartridge fuse panels utilize replaceable fuses for circuit protection, ideal for both residential and commercial applications. Aluminum wiring electrical panels cater to structures using aluminum wiring, ensuring compatibility and safety. Breaker panels, the most common type, employ circuit breakers that reset after tripping, offering convenience and enhanced safety. Each type addresses specific electrical needs and configurations in various settings.</p></p>
