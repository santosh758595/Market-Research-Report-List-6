<p><p>The Bluetooth audio chips and modules market comprises two main types: ordinary Bluetooth audio chips and Audio System-on-Chip (SoC) solutions. Ordinary Bluetooth audio chips are basic components that enable wireless audio transmission, providing essential functionality for devices like headphones and speakers. In contrast, Audio SoCs integrate multiple functions, including digital signal processing and audio codec, into a single chip, offering enhanced audio quality and power efficiency. This leads to more compact designs and improved performance in advanced audio applications.</p></p>
