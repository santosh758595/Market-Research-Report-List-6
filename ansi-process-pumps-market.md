<p><ul><li>Sealless</li><li>Sealded</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ANSI process pumps market is characterized by two primary types: sealless and sealed pumps. Sealless pumps utilize magnetic drive technology, eliminating the need for traditional mechanical seals, which reduces leakage risks and enhances safety, particularly in hazardous applications. Sealed pumps, on the other hand, employ mechanical seals to prevent fluid leakage, making them suitable for a wide range of industrial processes. Each type offers distinct advantages related to maintenance, efficiency, and environmental compliance, catering to diverse operational needs.</p></p>
