<p><p>The EPDM rubber market includes Modified Ethylene Propylene Rubber and Thermoplastic Ethylene-Propylene Rubber. Modified EPDM is chemically altered to enhance properties like weather resistance, flexibility, and durability, making it ideal for automotive seals, roofing membranes, and industrial applications. Thermoplastic EPDM combines rubber elasticity with thermoplastic processability, offering recyclability and ease of molding, suitable for automotive parts, consumer goods, and construction. Both types cater to diverse industries, balancing performance, cost-effectiveness, and environmental considerations.</p></p>
