<p><strong>The Atomic Magnetometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold atomic magnetometry</li><li>Spin-exchange relaxation-free (SERF) magnetometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The atomic magnetometer market includes various types, primarily cold atomic magnetometers and spin-exchange relaxation-free (SERF) magnetometers. Cold atomic magnetometers utilize ultra-cold atoms to achieve high sensitivity in magnetic field measurements, making them suitable for applications requiring precision. In contrast, SERF magnetometers enhance sensitivity by using spin-exchange interactions in alkali vapor, enabling operation in high magnetic fields. Both technologies are employed in fields like medical imaging, geophysical surveys, and magnetic field mapping, driving advancements in sensing capabilities.</p></p>
