<p><p>The artificial wood panel market encompasses various types, each serving distinct applications. Particleboard (PB) is made from wood chips and resin, offering an economical option for furniture. Medium Density Fibreboard (MDF) provides a smooth surface, making it ideal for cabinetry and moldings. High Density Fibreboard (HDF) is denser and stronger, used in flooring. Oriented Strand Board (OSB) consists of wood strands for structural applications. Plywood layers veneer for strength and versatility, while "Others" may include specialized composites or engineered wood products.</p></p>
