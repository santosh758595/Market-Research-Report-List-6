<p><p>Nitrile Butadiene Rubber (NBR) elastic gaskets come in various types, notably saddle and waveform designs. Saddle gaskets are characterized by their U-shaped cross-section, providing effective sealing for flanged joints. They are ideal for applications requiring a robust barrier against liquids and gases. Waveform gaskets, on the other hand, feature a series of wave-like patterns that enhance flexibility and compression recovery, making them suitable for dynamic sealing applications. Both types leverage NBR's excellent resistance to oils and chemicals, ensuring durability in various environments.</p></p>
