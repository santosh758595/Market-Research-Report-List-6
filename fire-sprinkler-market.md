<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Fire Sprinkler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Fire Sprinklers</li><li>Dry Fire Sprinklers</li><li>Deluge Fire Sprinklers</li><li>Preaction Fire Sprinklers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fire sprinkler market is categorized into four main types: </p><p>1. **Wet Fire Sprinklers**: These contain water under pressure, activating when heat generates a specified temperature, providing immediate response.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Dry Fire Sprinklers**: These use pressurized air or nitrogen to keep the water supply away from the piping until activated, suitable for cold environments.</p><p>3. **Deluge Fire Sprinklers**: These feature open heads that discharge water simultaneously over large areas, ideal for high-hazard environments.</p><p>4. **Preaction Fire Sprinklers**: Combining wet and dry systems, they require two triggers (heat and smoke) to release water, minimizing accidental discharge.</p></p>
