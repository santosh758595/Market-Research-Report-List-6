<p><p>Battery free RFID sensor market can be categorized into three types based on their operating frequencies. Low frequency RFID sensors operate at frequencies below 300 kHz and are ideal for short-range applications. High frequency RFID sensors operate at frequencies between 3-30 MHz and are commonly used for tracking and identification in various industries. Ultra high frequency RFID sensors operate at frequencies above 300 MHz and offer long read range and faster data transfer rates, making them suitable for applications requiring high-speed and long-distance communication.</p></p>
