<p><p>The liver cancer molecular diagnostics market encompasses several key techniques. PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) amplifies specific DNA sequences for detection of genetic abnormalities. FISH (Fluorescence In Situ Hybridization) identifies chromosomal abnormalities by tagging DNA probes with fluorescent dye. DNA sequencing offers detailed insights into genetic mutations and variations associated with liver cancer. Gene chips, or microarrays, analyze expression levels of thousands of genes simultaneously, providing a comprehensive view of tumor biology. Together, these methods enhance diagnosis and treatment strategies.</p></p>
