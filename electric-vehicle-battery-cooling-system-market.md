<p><p>The Electric Vehicle (EV) Battery Cooling System market includes two primary types: Air Cooling and Liquid Cooling Systems. Air Cooling Systems use airflow to dissipate heat from the battery, offering a simpler, cost-effective solution but with limited efficiency for high-performance EVs. Liquid Cooling Systems circulate coolant through channels around the battery, providing superior thermal management, higher efficiency, and better performance, especially in demanding conditions. Both systems aim to maintain optimal battery temperature, ensuring safety, longevity, and efficiency.</p></p>
