<p><p>The tactile feedback actuators market is primarily segmented into Eccentric Rotating Mass (ERM) actuators, Linear Resonant Actuators (LRAs), and other types. ERM actuators create vibrations by rotating an off-center mass, providing a robust tactile experience commonly used in smartphones. LRAs generate vibrations through linear motion, offering precise feedback and efficiency, making them popular in wearable devices. Other actuators include piezoelectric and electrostatic devices, which also enhance user interaction with varying levels of feedback intensity and responsiveness across applications.</p></p>
