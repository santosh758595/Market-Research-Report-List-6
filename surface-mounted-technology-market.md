<p><p>Surface Mounted Technology (SMT) market encompasses various types, including Single-sided Mixed Process and Double-sided Mixed Process. The Single-sided Mixed Process involves mounting components on one side of a printed circuit board (PCB), often simplifying production and assembly. In contrast, the Double-sided Mixed Process allows for components to be placed on both sides of the PCB, optimizing space and functionality for complex designs. Both methods cater to varying demands in electronics manufacturing, balancing efficiency and performance while supporting diverse applications.</p></p>
