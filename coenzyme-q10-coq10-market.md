<p><p>The Coenzyme Q10 (CoQ10) market is categorized into three types based on its oxidative states. Fully Oxidized (Ubiquinone) is the primary form, essential for energy production in mitochondria and commonly used in supplements. Semiquinone (Ubisemiquinone) is a transient state that plays a role in electron transfer but is less commercially prevalent. Fully Reduced (Ubiquinol) is the active antioxidant form, increasingly popular in supplements due to its higher bioavailability and health benefits, such as cardiovascular support and increased energy levels.</p></p>
