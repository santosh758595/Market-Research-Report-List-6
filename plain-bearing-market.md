<p><p>The plain bearing market encompasses various types, each serving unique applications. Journal bearings support rotating shafts, offering smooth motion in machinery. Linear bearings facilitate linear motion in guides and rails, ideal for automation and robotics. Thrust bearings manage axial loads, commonly used in automotive and industrial applications. Angular contact bearings support both axial and radial loads, providing stability in high-speed operations. Together, these types cater to diverse industries, ensuring efficient performance and reliability in mechanical systems.</p></p>
