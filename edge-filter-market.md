<p><strong>The Edge Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Long Pass Wave</li><li>Short Pass Wave</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Edge filters are optical devices that selectively transmit light in specific wavelength ranges. The Long Pass Wave filter allows wavelengths longer than a certain cutoff to pass through while attenuating shorter wavelengths, making it ideal for applications needing infrared light. Conversely, the Short Pass Wave filter permits wavelengths shorter than the cutoff to pass, blocking longer wavelengths, and is useful in applications requiring ultraviolet or visible light. Both types are essential in spectroscopy, photography, and various scientific applications.</p></p>
