Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) uses magnets and radio waves for high-resolution images, essential for evaluating complex cases. Ultrasonic equipment employs sound waves for real-time imaging, useful in distinguishing between solid tumors and cysts. Each modality plays a crucial role in effective breast cancer management.</p></p>
