<p><p>The ANC headset market comprises three main types: Full Cup (Circumaural), On-Ear (Supra Aural), and In-Ear (Intraaural). Full Cup headsets encompass the ears entirely, providing superior sound isolation and comfort, ideal for immersive listening. On-Ear headsets rest on the ears, offering a balance between portability and sound quality, suitable for casual use. In-Ear headsets fit directly into the ear canal, delivering excellent noise cancellation in a compact design, perfect for active lifestyles and travel.</p></p>
