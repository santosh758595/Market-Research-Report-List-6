<p><p>The Extrusion Coating Grade LDPE market consists of two primary production methods: the tubular process and the autoclave process. The tubular process involves melting LDPE and extruding it through a circular die to create a continuous tubular film, ideal for packaging applications. In contrast, the autoclave process uses a more controlled environment to polymerize LDPE, resulting in more uniform and high-quality coatings. Both methods cater to diverse applications, including food packaging, industrial uses, and consumer goods, impacting overall market dynamics.</p></p>
