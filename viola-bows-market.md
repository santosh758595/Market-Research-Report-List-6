Round bows, with a circular cross-section, provide a more rounded tone, ideal for smooth playability and warmth in sound. The "other" market includes unique bows made from various materials or specialized designs catering to niche preferences, allowing players to explore diverse tonal qualities and aesthetics.</p></p>
