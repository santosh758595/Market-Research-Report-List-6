<p><p>The automotive thick film resistors market is primarily segmented into two types: SMD (Surface Mount Device) and Through Hole types. SMD resistors are compact and designed for automated assembly, making them ideal for modern automotive applications where space is limited. In contrast, Through Hole resistors, with their leads inserted into drilled holes on a PCB, offer robust mechanical stability and are suitable for high-power applications. Both types serve critical roles in automotive electronics, ensuring performance and reliability in vehicle systems.</p></p>
