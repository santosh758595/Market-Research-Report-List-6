<p><p>Cardiac monitoring involves the continuous observation of heart activity to detect abnormalities in heart rhythm. Key market types include Holter monitors, which record heart activity over 24-48 hours; Implantable Loop Recorders (ILRs), used for long-term monitoring of intermittent arrhythmias; Event monitors that activate during symptoms; ECG monitors that provide real-time heart rhythm analysis; and general cardiac monitors that track overall heart health. These devices enhance diagnosis and management of cardiovascular conditions by providing valuable data to healthcare professionals.</p></p>
