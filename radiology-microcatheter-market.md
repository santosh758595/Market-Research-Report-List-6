<p><p>The Radiology Microcatheter market encompasses various types tailored for specific medical applications. Diagnostic microcatheters are designed for precise imaging and diagnosis by enabling targeted contrast agent delivery. Delivery microcatheters facilitate the placement of therapeutic agents directly into target tissues. Steerable microcatheters offer enhanced maneuverability, allowing detailed navigation through complex vascular systems. Aspiration microcatheters are employed to remove fluids or debris from specific areas, aiding in procedures that require tissue sampling or blockage clearance, thus enhancing treatment efficacy and patient outcomes.</p></p>
