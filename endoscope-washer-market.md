<p><strong>The Endoscope Washer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Chamber</li><li>Multi Chamber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The endoscope washer market is categorized into two main types: single chamber and multi-chamber systems. Single chamber washers streamline the reprocessing process by utilizing one compartment for cleaning and disinfecting, making them suitable for facilities with limited space or lower throughput needs. In contrast, multi-chamber washers offer multiple compartments for distinct cleaning, rinsing, and drying phases, ensuring thorough reprocessing of flexible endoscopes, which is essential for high-volume healthcare settings. Both types prioritize infection control and efficiency in endoscope maintenance.</p></p>
