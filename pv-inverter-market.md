<p><p>The PV inverter market consists of three main types: string inverters, central inverters, and microinverters. String inverters connect multiple solar panels in series, ideal for residential and small commercial systems. Central inverters consolidate power from larger arrays, commonly used in utility-scale installations due to their efficiency and cost-effectiveness. Microinverters attach to individual panels, maximizing energy capture and performance, particularly in shaded areas. Each type serves specific applications, influencing the market dynamics based on installation scale and energy needs.</p></p>
