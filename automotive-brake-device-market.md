<p><p>The automotive brake device market includes various advanced systems: **ACC (Adaptive Cruise Control)** adjusts speed to maintain a safe distance from vehicles ahead. **CMBS (Collision Mitigation Braking System)** detects potential collisions and applies brakes to reduce impact. **EBS (Electronic Braking System)** enhances braking efficiency through electronic control. **EBD (Electronic Brakeforce Distribution)** optimizes brake force between wheels for stability. **ESP (Electronic Stability Program)** prevents skidding by controlling individual brakes. **VSA (Vehicle Stability Assist)** maintains vehicle control during cornering or slippery conditions. These systems improve safety, performance, and driving dynamics.</p></p>
