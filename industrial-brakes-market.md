<p><p>The industrial brakes market can be categorized into three main types: disc brakes, drum brakes, and others. Disc brakes utilize a rotor and caliper system, offering efficient heat dissipation and superior stopping power, making them ideal for high-performance applications. Drum brakes operate using a brake drum and shoes, providing reliable performance for lower-speed applications. Other types may include magnetic, hydraulic, and electromechanical brakes, catering to specific industrial needs, enhancing safety, and efficiency in various machinery and equipment.</p></p>
