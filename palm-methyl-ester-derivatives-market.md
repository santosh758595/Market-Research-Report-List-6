<p><p>The palm methyl ester derivatives market consists primarily of two types: Crude Palm Oil (CPO) and Palm Kernel Oil (PKO). CPO, extracted from the flesh of palm fruits, is rich in saturated fats and is widely used in food products, cosmetics, and biofuels. In contrast, PKO, derived from the seeds of palm fruits, is higher in lauric acid and is commonly utilized in personal care products, detergents, and industrial applications. Both types play crucial roles in various industries.</p></p>
