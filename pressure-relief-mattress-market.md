<p><strong>The Pressure Relief Mattress Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Pressure Mattress</li><li>Alternating Pressure Mattress</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pressure relief mattress market encompasses two main types: static pressure mattresses and alternating pressure mattresses. Static pressure mattresses consist of high-density foam or gel that evenly distributes body weight to reduce pressure on vulnerable areas, making them ideal for long-term bedbound patients. In contrast, alternating pressure mattresses feature dynamic air cells that periodically inflate and deflate, promoting better blood circulation and preventing pressure sores. Both types serve essential roles in enhancing comfort and preventing complications in healthcare settings.</p></p>
