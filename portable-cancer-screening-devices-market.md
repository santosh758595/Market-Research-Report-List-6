<p><p>The Portable Cancer Screening Devices market includes technologies like Infrared Thermography and Electrical Impedance Spectroscopy. Infrared Thermography detects temperature variations on the skin's surface, potentially indicating underlying tumors. It is non-invasive and provides quick results. Electrical Impedance Spectroscopy, on the other hand, measures the electrical properties of tissues to differentiate between healthy and malignant cells. This technique offers real-time analysis and is also non-invasive. Both devices aim to enhance early cancer detection and improve patient outcomes through portable, accessible technology.</p></p>
