<p><ul><li>Multifocal Intraocular Lenses</li><li>Adjustable Intraocular Lenses</li><li>Aspheric Intraocular Lenses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The intraocular lenses market comprises various types designed to enhance vision post-cataract surgery. Multifocal intraocular lenses provide multiple focal points, allowing clear vision at different distances, reducing the need for glasses. Adjustable intraocular lenses offer patients the ability to fine-tune their vision after implantation using light, accommodating changes in visual needs. Aspheric intraocular lenses are designed to minimize optical distortions and provide improved contrast sensitivity, delivering sharper images, especially in low-light conditions, enhancing overall visual quality.</p></p>
