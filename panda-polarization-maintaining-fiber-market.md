<p><p>Panda polarization maintaining fiber is categorized into two main types: Single Mode and Multimode. Single Mode fibers allow only one light path, maintaining polarization over long distances, making them ideal for applications requiring high precision, such as telecommunications. Conversely, Multimode fibers support multiple light paths, making them suitable for shorter distances and applications where bandwidth and data rate are essential, such as local area networks. Both types enable effective control of light polarization for various industries, including aerospace and medical imaging.</p></p>
