<p><p>Automotive Event Data Recorders (EDRs) are categorized into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I EDRs capture and store data about pre-crash events, including vehicle speed, braking, and steering patterns, primarily focusing on accident scenarios. In contrast, Type II EDRs have advanced capabilities, documenting a broader range of information during regular vehicle operation, including driving behavior and system performance.
