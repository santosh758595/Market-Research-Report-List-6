<p><p>The insulated cable and wire market includes various types, each serving unique applications. XLPE (cross-linked polyethylene) insulated cables are known for their excellent thermal and electrical properties, making them ideal for power transmission. Mineral insulated cables utilize a copper or aluminum core surrounded by a mineral insulation, providing high fire resistance and durability, suitable for harsh environments. Alloy mineral insulated cables combine metal and mineral insulation for enhanced performance, offering lightweight and corrosion-resistant solutions for specialized industrial applications.</p></p>
