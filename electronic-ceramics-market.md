<p><strong>The Electronic Ceramics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ferroelectric</li><li>Piezoelectric</li><li>Pyroelectric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic ceramics market is categorized into three main types: ferroelectric, piezoelectric, and pyroelectric ceramics. Ferroelectric ceramics exhibit spontaneous polarization, allowing them to be used in memory devices and capacitors. Piezoelectric ceramics generate an electric charge in response to mechanical stress, making them essential in sensors and actuators. Pyroelectric ceramics produce a voltage when exposed to temperature changes, commonly utilized in infrared sensors and thermal imaging. These materials find extensive applications across various industries, driving market growth.</p></p>
