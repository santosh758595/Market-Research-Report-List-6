<p><p>Epoxy resin for marine composites is available in three main types: single component, double component, and multi component. Single component epoxy resin is pre-mixed and ready to use, making it convenient for small-scale applications. Double component epoxy resin requires mixing two components together before application, providing more control over curing time. Multi component epoxy resin involves mixing three or more components, offering advanced properties and customization options for specific marine composite projects. Each type has its own advantages and is suited for different applications within the marine industry.</p></p>
